About Tensor AI


Tensor AI is a software company specializing in applied artificial intelligence, specifically Neural-Networks. Our technology employs state-of-the-art algorithms and theory to achieve world class, unrivaled results.

Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to provide custom solutions for business problems through applied aritificial intelligence. These solutions are specifically relevant to businesses which seek to enhance their efficiency on routine data-backed tasks which previously required human interaction/interpretation.
